Tournament Guidelines
  1. The participant must have a high elo rank in order to join the tournament.
  2. Players must be 18 years old and above.
  3. Each team will only have 6 players each, including the substitute/s.
  4. The production team will provide particular devices/controllers for the usage of each player, other devices/controllers are not allowed.
  5. Show respect to your teammates, opponents, and event staff.
  6. Hate speech, offensive behavior, and any type of violence are prohibited. Two warnings will be given. The third warning will be a sign of disqualification for the entire team.
  7. Each player has the right to notify the tournament referee to pause the game when there is a defect noticed at any kind.
  8. Matchmaking will be done fairly. Complains will be addressed accordingly.
  9. Game cheats are strictly prohibited. Once known that a player uses such, they will not be able to continue playing throughout the tournament.
  10. Enjoy. Play fair.
